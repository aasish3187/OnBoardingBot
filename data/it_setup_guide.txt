CHAPTER 3: NETWORK & VPN CONFIGURATION
================================================================================

OFFICE NETWORK:
1. Connect to Wi-Fi network: "NexusTech-Corp"
2. Authentication: Use your domain credentials (firstname.lastname + password)
3. The network will auto-configure proxy settings
4. Speed: Up to 500 Mbps symmetric

GUEST NETWORK:
- Network: "NexusTech-Guest"
- For personal devices and visitors only
- No access to internal resources

VPN SETUP (FOR REMOTE WORK):
The company uses Cisco AnyConnect VPN for secure remote access.

Step-by-step VPN setup:
1. Download Cisco AnyConnect from the Software Center (Start Menu > Software
   Center) or from https://vpn.nexustech.com
2. Install Cisco AnyConnect Secure Mobility Client
3. Launch AnyConnect and enter the VPN server address: vpn.nexustech.com
4. Click "Connect"
5. Enter your credentials:
   - Username: firstname.lastname
   - Password: your domain password
   - Second factor: Enter the OTP from your Microsoft Authenticator app
6. Click "OK" to establish the connection
7. You should see "Connected to vpn.nexustech.com" in the AnyConnect window

VPN USAGE GUIDELINES:
- VPN must be connected whenever working remotely
- VPN provides access to all internal resources (Jira, Confluence, Git, etc.)
- Split tunneling is enabled — personal browsing goes through your ISP
- VPN sessions auto-disconnect after 12 hours of inactivity
- If you experience slow VPN speeds, try connecting to the alternate server:
  vpn2.nexustech.com

TROUBLESHOOTING VPN:
- "Authentication failed": Reset your password via the self-service portal
  (https://passwordreset.nexustech.com)
- "Connection timeout": Check your internet connection; try a different network
- "Certificate error": Update Cisco AnyConnect to the latest version from
  Software Center

CHAPTER 7: PASSWORD POLICY & TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
================================================================================

PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS:
Your password must meet ALL of the following criteria:
- Minimum 12 characters long
- At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least 1 number (0-9)
- At least 1 special character (!@#$%^&*)
- Cannot be any of your last 10 passwords
- Cannot contain your name, username, or common dictionary words

PASSWORD MANAGEMENT:
- Passwords expire every 90 days — you will receive a reminder 7 days before
- Use 1Password (company password manager) to generate and store passwords
- NEVER share your password with anyone, including IT staff
- NEVER write passwords on sticky notes or store in plain text files

PASSWORD RESET:
If you forget your password:
1. Go to https://passwordreset.nexustech.com
2. Enter your username (firstname.lastname)
3. Verify your identity via your registered mobile number or backup email
4. Create a new password meeting the requirements above
5. If self-service fails, contact IT Helpdesk (Ext. 2001)

TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA) SETUP:
2FA is MANDATORY for all employees. Set it up within your first day.

Step-by-step 2FA setup:
1. Download Microsoft Authenticator app on your mobile phone
   - iOS: App Store > search "Microsoft Authenticator"
   - Android: Google Play > search "Microsoft Authenticator"
2. Go to https://aka.ms/mfasetup from your laptop
3. Sign in with your company credentials
4. Select "Mobile app" as the verification method
5. Click "Set up" — a QR code will appear on screen
6. Open Microsoft Authenticator on your phone
7. Tap "+" > "Work or school account" > "Scan QR code"
8. Scan the QR code displayed on your laptop screen
9. A 6-digit code will appear in the app — enter it on the website to verify
10. Click "Done" — 2FA is now active

WHEN 2FA IS REQUIRED:
- VPN login (always)
- Email access from new devices
- HRMS portal login
- Jira/Confluence from non-company networks
- Password changes

BACKUP METHODS:
- Register a backup phone number for SMS-based OTP
- Save the recovery codes provided during setup (store in 1Password)
- If you lose your phone, contact IT Helpdesk for temporary bypass (Ext. 2001)

CHAPTER 8: DATA SECURITY & ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
================================================================================

CLASSIFICATION OF DATA:
- PUBLIC: Marketing materials, website content, press releases
- INTERNAL: Employee handbook, internal presentations, meeting notes
- CONFIDENTIAL: Client data, financial reports, HR records, source code
- RESTRICTED: Encryption keys, API secrets, customer PII, health records

DATA HANDLING RULES:
1. Never store CONFIDENTIAL or RESTRICTED data on personal devices
2. Use company-approved cloud storage only (OneDrive, SharePoint, Google Drive)
3. Encrypt sensitive files before sharing externally (use 7-Zip with AES-256)
4. Never email passwords, API keys, or access tokens
5. Lock your laptop (Win+L / Cmd+Control+Q) when leaving your desk

ACCEPTABLE USE:
- Company devices are primarily for business use
- Limited personal use is permitted (browsing, personal email) during breaks
- Streaming services, gaming, and torrenting are blocked on the corporate network
- All internet traffic on company devices is monitored and logged

SECURITY INCIDENT REPORTING:
If you suspect a security breach or incident:
1. DO NOT try to investigate or fix it yourself
2. Report immediately to: security@nexustech.com
3. Call the Security Operations Center (SOC): Ext. 2005
4. Preserve evidence — do not delete files, emails, or logs

CHAPTER 10: TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON ISSUES
================================================================================

ISSUE: LAPTOP WON'T TURN ON
→ Ensure the charger is connected and the power light is on
→ Try a hard reset: Hold power button for 15 seconds, then release and press
  again
→ If still not working, contact IT Helpdesk for replacement

ISSUE: CANNOT CONNECT TO WI-FI
→ Ensure you're connecting to "NexusTech-Corp" (not "NexusTech-Guest")
→ Forget the network and re-connect with your domain credentials
→ Restart your laptop and try again
→ If in a dead zone, use the Ethernet port on your docking station

ISSUE: OUTLOOK NOT SYNCING
→ Check your internet connection
→ Restart Outlook
→ Clear the Outlook cache: File > Account Settings > Data Files > Delete
  OST file (it will re-download)
→ If still failing, run the Microsoft Support and Recovery Assistant (SaRA)

ISSUE: VPN CONNECTION DROPS
→ Check your internet stability (try speedtest.net)
→ Try the alternate VPN server: vpn2.nexustech.com
→ Disable any personal VPN or proxy software
→ Update Cisco AnyConnect to the latest version

ISSUE: SLOW LAPTOP PERFORMANCE
→ Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications
→ Restart your laptop (restart clears memory leaks)
→ Check for Windows/macOS updates
→ If persistent, IT may need to re-image your device

ISSUE: 2FA NOT WORKING
→ Ensure your phone's time is set to automatic
→ Force-close and reopen Microsoft Authenticator
→ If codes are always "invalid", your app may be out of sync — remove and
  re-add your account in the Authenticator app
→ For emergency access, call IT Helpdesk for a temporary bypass code
